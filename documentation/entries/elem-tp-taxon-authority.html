<tp:taxon-authority>

Elements that are linked to the name in the original treatment of a taxon as designated by the authors of the respective original publication. ~ ~

Attributes

  • @id
  • @role
  • @xml:base

Content Model

( #PCDATA | object-id | tp:taxon-authority-part )*

This can be anything from a single author to a whole string including author, year, publication page, etc.. Formica rufa L. or Formica rufa Linnaeus 1758:580 ~ ~~

May Contain:

  • PCDATA
  • <object-id>
  • <tp:taxon-authority-part>

May be contained in:

  • <tp:mixed-nomenclature>
  • <tp:nomenclature>
  • <tp:nomenclature-citation>
